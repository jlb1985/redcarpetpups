RITZY CANINE CARRIAGE HOUSE

New York, NY

The Ritzy Canine Carriage House rivals New York City's swankiest hotels, but, of course, this one is designed just for pets. A pet owner can pay up to $50 a night for their pooch to stay here. The Ritzy Canine Carriage House features a rooftop play area with a garden and a waterfall. At night, dogs can stay in a regular suite or the "Presidential Suite," which has a bed, toy chest, and television. When not playing on the roof, guests can spend time in the indoor play area or at the spa. Their other option is to browse the gift shop, where they can peruse pet items, from dog prom dresses to designer dog carriers.

ELEMENT HOTELS

Scottsdale, AZ

According to BringFido users, Element Hotels was the top dog among pet-friendly hotel chains in the U.S. last year. Belonging to the Marriott family of properties, the popular chain has created a nature-inspired look and feel that suits today’s canine traveler. Accommodations with spa-inspired bathrooms and fully equipped kitchens make Element ideal for short or long stays with your canine companion. Furry guests are welcome at more than 75 locations across America, many of which don't charge a pet fee. Element Hotels’ “Love That Dog” program provides special amenities for visiting pups, including a plush bed, food and water bowls, and waste bags for easy cleanup.

LOEWS HOTELS

Tucson, AZ

Loews Hotels knows how to treat pets and their owners. Establishing itself as one of the leading luxury pet-friendly hotel chains in the world, visitors can experience the high life at more than two dozen locations across the U.S. and Canada. And every Loews is a true original, incorporating the local flavor of each destination into its design. Loews’ canine guests are pampered with everything from creature comforts like treats, bowls, and place mats at check-in to pet beds, leashes, and collars available on request. The hotel chain even offers a gourmet room service menu just for cats or dogs.

GRADUATE HOTELS

Lincoln, NE

Graduate Hotels ranks near the top of the class on our list of best pet-friendly hotel chains. Located in college towns across the U.S. and the U.K., each of its more than 30 boutique hotels showcases local collegiate culture and charm. Guests appreciate the well-designed decor, warm atmosphere and luxury touches like Malin + Goetz bath products in the rooms and Peloton bikes in the on-site gym. And the hotel extends a special welcome to furry friends. While pet fees and weight restrictions differ from property to property, certain locations feature on-site dog parks with waste stations, and many provide tasty dog treats.
